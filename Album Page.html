American Eyes

Hilltribe

2011

American eyes is an EP released by the musical group Hilltribe.
This EP would consist of the last songs ever released by Hilltribe,
including Third World Girl and I Can Do Anything

Third World Girl is a ballad based on an international love story.
As soon as the hook hits you can't help but sing a long to the melody.
I Can Do Anything is another ballad based on the the struggles and
triumphs each member has gone through.
