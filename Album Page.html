American Eyes

Hilltribe

2011

American eyes is an EP released by the musical group Hilltribe.
This EP would consist of the last songs ever released by Hilltribe,
including Third World Girl, I Can Do Anything and Fong Lee.

Band Members and Producers

  • Doua Vang-Vocalist
  • Tria Vang-Vocalist
  • Vong Lee-Vocalist
  • Produced by Hilltribe
